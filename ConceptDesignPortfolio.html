Casey's house

This is the house of the main character for my personal project, a comic called The Best Orphanage. This design is inspired by my grandmother's place but also with a little bit of imagination. Casey is a 30-year-old woman who lives in a very tiny house fit for only 1 person with her beloved garden just right next to it.

Casey full reference design

Casey has a very simple design, she is one of the main characters in The Best Orphanage. her design is inspired by my favorite dress but in a different color. she is also inspired by my admiration for strong-minded women, and this is how I picture that description and personality.

Old man turnaround design

One of the main characters in my personal comic The Best Orphanage. is the caretaker for the orphanage that takes care of the most rare and mysterious individuals on earth. his design is just what I picture an orphanage caretaker would look like in a fantasy setting. with his robes and his headband that describe the sunset and tides.

Pikku turnaround design
